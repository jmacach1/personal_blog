Oasis Of The Mind

By Jerald Macachor
04/16/2014

Here we stand on an ocean of sand
An oasis of our own design
While we ride on this pyramid slide
Taking the treasures we find

Golden tooth enamel on a back of a camel
Trinkets and what's-its galore
Genie in a bottle, magic carpet full throttle
Up in the clouds we soar

Feeling vibrations with the constellations
Oh What a sight to behold
The planets align forming a conga line
And limbo under the asteroids

Exotic places, and a sea of happy faces
The world is an open door
Despite the all wonders it seems to offer
You're still all I want to explore

In a lovers embrace we'll reach old age
Swallowed by the sands of time
Though our bones will bleach our souls will reach
A heavenly state of mind
